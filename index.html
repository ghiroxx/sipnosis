Our Mission

Why we exist and what we refuse to let disappear.

Flavors Forgotten documents recipes that carry memory, identity, resistance, and dignity. We record them while the original voices are still alive.

This is not nostalgia content. It's cultural survival. We capture ingredients, technique, dialect, ritual context, who taught it, and what was lost along the way.

We're building an archive — and proof that these people, and their food, were here.
